The Golden Age of The Internet
Yearning for a time I only got a glimpse of
Cameron Lee, 03/12/2026 - 03:44pm
Preface

If you've ever gotten to know me, you will quickly see how much appreciation I have for media and tech from the early 2000s. I collect CD's to play in my 2008 Mercedes, burn mixtapes for my friends, swapped my smart watch for an old school CASIO, and absolutely adore the stylistic choices of the era. I could go on and on about this but I'll save that for another blog post. This blog is dedicated to one specific part of that era which I see as the
Golden Age
of the internet, a period where I was fortunate enough to catch the tail end of but was not old enough to fully enjoy.
