APEocalypse
2D auto-battler created by me!
Cameron Lee, 11/04/2025 - 09:04pm
Preface

This semester at SDSU, I'm taking CS583: 3D Game Programming. This class has been a blast so far and has been really cool to get industry insights from Christopher Fodor, a current employee at Sony Entertainment with tons of experience in the game industry. And, while this class is called
3D
Game Programming, before you can run you must walk!
The Concept
Ah, where every good project begins....the concept. I originally came up with APEocalypse, largely as a joke, last year, while chatting with some friends. A new meme had been making its rounds at the time, discussing who would win in a battle: 100 Unarmed Men vs 1 Gorilla. Now while I am no expert on primates, I personally feel like 100 men could easily take down one gorilla. With this in mind I proposed a more interesting question to my friends:

Would 10 men
of your choice be able to take on a gorilla?


This question got plenty of discussion revolving. Could 10x Mike Tyson's fight a gorilla? What about having olympic distance runners to tire out the gorilla? Would the inclusion of an animal expert giving guidance and direction make a difference?

From this question emerged another. Assuming that your perfect team of 10 people could defeat a gorilla, just
how many
gorillas could they defeat?
Enter APEocalypse
The concept is simple, survive as many waves as you can
